# Starter Local AI Apps

Actually useful Local AI apps + extensions for many use cases without much tinkering.

## Why this repo exists?

AI is going nowhere, indifferent to its status quo being a bubble or not. So let's make it our own! Here's a collecion of many applications that are **stupidly simple** to install & use, powered by *local AI* excluding [AIaaS - AI-as-a-Service](https://www.ibm.com/think/topics/ai-as-a-service-aiaas) providers.

I was in search for quality open-source AI applications OR applications that utilize local AI to an extent which they gracefully integrate within their way of use cases.

These apps require near-zero tinkering for beginners, I hope.

> I intend to keep this repo up-to-date. - me :3

## Why you should choose Local AI over AIaaS?

If you [value your privacy](https://cambridgeanalytica.org/news/the-birth-of-surveillance-capitalism-how-cambridge-analytica-exposed-the-business-of-human-data-3583/), if you oppose [technocratic takeover](https://www.codastory.com/polarization/can-we-trust-an-ai-jury-to-judge-journalism/), if you reject [self-proclaimed entitled brats' own dictation onto you](https://www.cato-unbound.org/2009/04/13/peter-thiel/education-libertarian/), if you [reject inhumane use cases of AI](https://www.reuters.com/technology/pentagon-adopt-palantir-ai-as-core-us-military-system-memo-says-2026-03-20/), if you love [open-source](https://thelibre.news/foss-infrastructure-is-under-attack-by-ai-companies/), if you reject [intellectual enshittification](https://www.bloodinthemachine.com/p/if-ai-is-writing-the-work-and-ai), if you value [human-provided original knowledge](https://archive.is/GoVol), if you love [open web](https://www.baldurbjarnason.com/2024/the-deskilling-of-web-dev-is-harming-us-all/), and [intent to keep it open](https://www.eff.org/deeplinks/2026/03/blocking-internet-archive-wont-stop-ai-it-will-erase-webs-historical-record), if you want to be [aware of your future](https://tanyaverma.sh/2026/04/10/closing-of-the-frontier.html) and basically want to **own your AI.** 

As a *prosumer* it is your natural right to be aware what you consume & keep the capital in check with your demands.
